-Balance Chart
	Displaying a line chart with each week with the following options: 7 days, 1 month, 2 months, 3 months, 6 months, 1 year.

-Cash Flow
	Displays the income and the expenses for that month, then a results by calculating the difference between the two for that month.

-Monthly Charges
	Displaying a donut chart with all the monthly charges that will be made up to 8 (e.g. Broadband, Phone, Insurance, Fuel, Rent etc )

-Transactions List
	Just a card view list of all the transactions made in that month, have the option to select to display transactions as old as: options: 7 days, 1 month, 2 months, 3 months, 6 months, 1 year, 2 years and 5 years.

-Weekly Spendings
	Display a bar chart with 4 weeks containing the amount of transactions made in the respective week.
